Our Curriculum

Programs Offered

Structured, conceptual, and personalized coaching for Grades 7 to 10 in Maths and Science.

07

Grade 7 – Foundation Maths & Science

Focus on strong fundamentals of Maths and Science.

This program is designed to remove the fear of mathematics and science by building a very strong foundation. We cover basic arithmetic, introductory algebra, geometry, and core scientific concepts such as physics fundamentals (motion, force, energy), basic chemistry (atoms, reactions, everyday applications), and life science (human body, plants, and ecosystems) through highly interactive and visual teaching techniques to ensure that students actually enjoy solving problems.

  • ✓ Number System & Algebra Basics
  • ✓ Visual Geometry
  • ✓ Mental Math Strategies
08

Grade 8 – Concept Builder

Maths and Science clarity.

Grade 8 bridges foundational learning to high school rigor. We heavily focus on conceptual clarity in both Maths and Science, introducing students to physics and chemistry fundamentals using real-world examples.

  • ✓ Advanced Algebra & Linear Equations
  • ✓ Introduction to Physics & Chemistry
  • ✓ Conceptual Worksheets
09

Grade 9 – Advanced Learning

Preparation for complex topics and analytical thinking.

The syllabus significantly jumps in complexity. Our Grade 9 program trains students to think analytically. We break down complex theorems, scientific principles, and chemical equations so students truly understand the 'why' behind the 'how'.

  • ✓ Complex Problem Solving
  • ✓ Analytical Geometry & Trigonometry
  • ✓ Foundational Board Prep
10

Grade 10 – Board Mastery

Full board exam preparation including tests and revision.

A dedicated, high-focus program for board exam candidates. We provide structured preparation that includes comprehensive syllabus coverage, previous year question paper analysis, rigorous mock tests, and personalized revision plans.

  • ✓ Full Syllabus Coverage by November
  • ✓ Weekly Mock Tests & Feedback
  • ✓ Last 10 Years Paper Solving
